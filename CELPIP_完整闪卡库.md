#### CLB 7 - 论文基础结构 (8张)

**卡片11: 论文5段结构框架**
```
Front:

Back:

**卡片12: 论文高分开场 - Intro**
```
Front:
Task 2引入段高分写法 (避免5个常见错误)

Back:
错误1: 直接复制题目
❌ Some people believe that technology has a 
   positive impact on society. I agree with this 
   statement.
✅ The role of technology in shaping modern 
   society is undeniable. While some argue that 
   it poses challenges, the overwhelming evidence 
   suggests that technological advancement has 
   enriched human life significantly.

错误2: Intro太长
❌ [写了半页纸]
✅ 30-40字，简洁有力

错误3: 没有明确立场
❌ This topic is important and people have 
   different opinions.
✅ This development is overwhelmingly beneficial 
   for the following reasons...

错误4: 论据不清晰
❌ I will discuss various points about this issue
✅ Two key reasons support this position: improved 
   efficiency and enhanced accessibility

错误5: 语言平凡
❌ I think... / In my opinion... / I believe...
✅ The evidence strongly suggests... / It is 
   undeniable that... / This development is 
   clearly beneficial because...
```

**卡片16: 论文常见错误1 - 逻辑错误**
```
Front:
Task 2常见逻辑错误

Back:
错误1: 论据与论点不符
❌ "Technology is bad. For example, smartphones 
   help people communicate faster."
✅ 例子必须支持论点

错误2: 论段之间重复
❌ Para 2: Technology improves efficiency
   Para 3: Technology improves efficiency (换个例子)
✅ 每段论点不同

错误3: 论据不充分
❌ "Online education is good because I like it"
✅ "Online education improves accessibility for 
   rural students who have limited access to 
   physical institutions"

错误4: 逻辑不连贯
❌ "First, technology is useful. Second, I have 
   a car."
✅ 所有观点都应围绕中心论题

错误5: 立场不一致
❌ Intro说支持，Body说有问题，Conclusion又说反对
✅ 全文立场必须一致
```

## 👂 听力闪卡 (35张)

### 关键词识别 (15张)

**卡片26: Listening Part 4&5关键词预测**
```
Front:
如何在听力前预测关键信息 (Pre-listening)

Back:
关键词预测的3个步骤:

第1步: 读题目，找问题类型
Q: "What is the main purpose of..."
→ 关键词: purpose, main idea

Q: "Why does the speaker mention..."
→ 关键词: reason, because, since

Q: "What can be inferred about..."
→ 关键词: suggest, indicate, imply

第2步: 识别关键词范围
针对Part 4演讲:
- 开头通常有主题陈述 (Main point)
